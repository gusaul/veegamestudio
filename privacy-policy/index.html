Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Vee Game Studio ("we," "us," or "our") develops casual and puzzle games. This Privacy Policy governs your use of our software applications ("Applications") on iOS and Android. Our primary goal is to provide a fun experience while being transparent about the data we interact with.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect "Personally Identifiable Information" (like your real name or home address) directly. However, we use third-party SDKs that collect data to help us run the game.

Type of Data Service Provider Purpose
Device ID (IDFA/AAID), IP Address Google AdMob Advertising & Attribution
Usage Stats, Level Progression Firebase Analytics Internal Research & Optimization
Player Alias, Scores, Achievements Apple Game Center / Google Play Games Social Features & Leaderboards
Transaction ID, Purchase History Apple / Google In-App Purchase Fulfillment of "Diamonds"

3. Use of Cookies and Advertising

Our advertising partners (AdMob) may use cookies or mobile advertising identifiers to deliver ads. You can opt out of interest-based advertising in your device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads" on Android).

4. Data Retention and Security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Analytics data stored by Firebase is kept for a period determined by their policies (typically 14 months). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our applications.

5. International Compliance (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are a resident of the EEA or California, you have the right to access, delete, or limit the processing of your data. Since we do not have accounts, you can exercise these rights by resetting your Advertising ID or uninstalling our games.

Questions? Contact us at support@veegame.studio.
